About ESSC Portal

The ESSC Portal aggregates information about product builds and releases in one central place.
It's goal is to provide all relevant information about every product going through ESSC team's hands.

The ESSC Portal is easily editable by anyone. Yet it gathers some information automatically.

If you have any suggestion on how to make ESSC Portal better, let us know.

About ESSC

The ESSC team is a joint point between JBoss development, productization and QA.
Our goal is to ensure that productization team gets a stable and testable project as an input. Also, our goal is to catalyze communication of needs and intends between teams.
